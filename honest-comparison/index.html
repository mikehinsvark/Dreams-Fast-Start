The DBR Difference

Every business model on this site has been evaluated through the same lens: Money, Market, Moat, and Machine. These four pillars determine whether a business creates real, lasting wealth — or just keeps you busy.

The best businesses score high on Money (recurring, vested, fast-paid), Market (big TAM + urgent pain + tailwind), Moat (differentiator that's hard to copy), and Machine (duplicatable system that retains clients). DBR nails all four — with $0 to start, no employees, no inventory, and renewals for life.
